Regional Weather Summary for the High Plains
National Weather Service Goodland KS
153 PM MDT Sun Oct 23 2022

Strong winds and blowing dust have been present across the area.
Temperatures as of 1pm ranged from 75 in Flagler, Colorado to 89
in Hill City, Kansas. Winds have generally been from the southwest
at 25 to 40 mph with gusts of 40 to 60 mph.

OUTLOOK... The remainder of the afternoon will see the strong
winds and blowing dust continue until a cold front moves through
around dinner time. The winds will then shift to out of the
northwest and the blowing dust will diminish from north to south.
The overnight hours will see winds around 15-25 mph with a mix of
clouds and clear skies. Lows will drop into the 30's and 40's.
Tomorrow, much cooler with highs in the 50's and skies clearing
through the day. Winds will remain gusty from the north with gusts
up to 30 mph.

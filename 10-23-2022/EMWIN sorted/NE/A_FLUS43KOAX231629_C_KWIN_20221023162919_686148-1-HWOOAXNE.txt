.DAY ONE...This Afternoon and Tonight

A storm system will bring strong south winds, thunderstorms, and 
fire weather concerns to the region today and tonight.

A Wind Advisory is in effect for most of the region through 2 AM 
Monday. Peak wind gusts near 50 mph are possible through most of 
the day, with higher gusts in the 50 to 60 mph range possible 
between 4 PM and midnight. Travelers may experience strong 
crosswinds or areas of blowing dust. Consider securing any loose 
Halloween decorations.

There is a slight risk for severe thunderstorms today, with the
greatest risk expected between 6 PM and midnight. The primary
threat with any storms will be damaging wind gusts to 70 mph. 
Quarter size hail may also occur.

A Red Flag Warning remains in place for portions of Nebraska and
Iowa through 8 PM today. Strong winds, dry fuels and low relative
humidity will result in an extreme fire danger.

.DAYS TWO THROUGH SEVEN...Monday through Saturday

The probability for hazardous weather is low.

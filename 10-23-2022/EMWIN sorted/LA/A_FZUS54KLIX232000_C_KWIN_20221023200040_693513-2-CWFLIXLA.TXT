.SYNOPSIS FOR PASCAGOULA TO ATCHAFALAYA RIVER OUT 60 NM...

High pressure continues to work off to the northeast and a surface
low will begin to develop over the Red Rvier Valley tomorrow. Winds
are already veering around to the southeast and will continue to do
so through Monday. By Monday night and Tuesday the deepening surface
low will move across Arkansas and towards the Ohio Valley Tuesday
night. Ahead of this low look for strong southerly winds with
possible small craft advisory conditions Tuesday.

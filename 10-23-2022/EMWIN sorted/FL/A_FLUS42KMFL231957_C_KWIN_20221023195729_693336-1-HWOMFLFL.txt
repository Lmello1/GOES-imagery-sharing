This Hazardous Weather Outlook is for Atlantic, Biscayne Bay, South 
Florida, Gulf of Mexico and Lake Okeechobee.

.DAY ONE...This afternoon and tonight.

Rip Currents: A high rip current risk remains along the Palm Beaches
today. The remaining Atlantic beaches will also see an elevated rip 
current risk.

Marine: Hazardous marine conditions in the form of higher seas and 
winds, mainly across the Atlantic waters. Cautionary conditions will
continue today.

.DAYS TWO THROUGH SEVEN...Monday through Saturday.

A high risk of rip currents will remain for the Palm Beaches through
Monday. An elevated risk of rip currents will remain for the rest of
the Atlantic beaches.

Elevated high tide cycles are expected this week, which may result
in some minor coastal flooding across vulnerable Atlantic coast 
locations.

High surf conditions could impact Palm Beach county beaches on Monday
due to increased swell in the Atlantic waters. 

Hazardous marine conditions could continue into the upcoming week.

This hazardous weather outlook is for portions of southeast
Arkansas, northeast Louisiana, central Mississippi, north central
Mississippi, and south central Mississippi.

.DAY ONE...Tonight and Monday

The probability for widespread hazardous weather is low.

.DAYS TWO THROUGH SEVEN...Monday night through Saturday

Thunderstorms will move across the forecast area on Tuesday ahead
of a cold front. Some of these storms could be severe with
damaging winds, tornadoes, and small hail possible.

.SPOTTER CALL TO ACTION STATEMENT...

.DISCUSSION...

A deep low pressure system will cross north through Minnesota
tonight. Winds will strengthen out of the south today with gusts 
in excess of 30 mph at times. Winds will continue to strengthen 
into Monday and some possible wind advisories may be needed 
across the Arrowhead and northern WI. Min RHs are expected to stay
fairly high in the 40s. Thunderstorms are also expected and some
of them could be strong overnight west of Duluth though large hail
is the main threat. the remainder of the forecast is quite benign
with light winds, cooler temperatures, and MinRH in the 40s and
50s.

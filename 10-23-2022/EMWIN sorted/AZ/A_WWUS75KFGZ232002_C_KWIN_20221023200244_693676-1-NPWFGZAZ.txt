...FREEZE WARNING REMAINS IN EFFECT FROM 9 PM THIS EVENING TO
8 AM MST MONDAY...

* WHAT...Sub-freezing temperatures as low as 23 expected.

* WHERE...Most elevations above 5000 feet across northern Arizona.

* WHEN...From 9 PM this evening to 8 AM MST Monday.

* IMPACTS...Frost and freeze conditions will kill sensitive 
  vegetation and possibly damage unprotected outdoor plumbing.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

Take steps now to protect tender plants from the cold. To prevent
freezing and possible bursting of outdoor water pipes they should
be wrapped, drained, or allowed to drip slowly. Those that have
in-ground sprinkler systems should drain them and cover above-
ground pipes to protect them from freezing.

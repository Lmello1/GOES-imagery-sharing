.DISCUSSION...
Breezy south winds will gradually weaken to around 10 to 15 mph 
this evening before strengthening again by mid-morning Monday. Our
next front will move through the area Monday night into Tuesday, 
bringing potential for rain and thunderstorms ahead and along the 
boundary. Storms should be diminishing in strength as they cross 
the area; however, the strongest of the storms could be capable of
producing damaging winds as they enter the area from the 
northwest. Once the front passes, winds will become northwesterly,
remaining gusty into early Tuesday afternoon. Though winds will 
gradually die down, drier and cooler air will also make its way 
into the area.

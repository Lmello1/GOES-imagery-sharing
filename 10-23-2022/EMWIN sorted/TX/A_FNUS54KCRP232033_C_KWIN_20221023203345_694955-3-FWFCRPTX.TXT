National Weather Service Corpus Christi TX
333 PM CDT Sun Oct 23 2022

.DISCUSSION...
Scattered showers and isolated thunderstorms are expected Monday 
night ahead and along of a cold front that will move across South 
Texas Monday night. Drier conditions are expected Tuesday afternoon 
through Wednesday with minimum relative humidity dropping into the 
20-35% range for all of South Texas except for the Coastal Bend, 
where relative humidity values will remain in the 40-50% range. 
However, winds should remain below criteria during this time, 
negating elevated fire weather concerns. Onshore flow will return 
Wednesday night and moisture will gradually increase Thursday and 
Friday. Elevated fire weather conditions are not anticipated at 
this time.

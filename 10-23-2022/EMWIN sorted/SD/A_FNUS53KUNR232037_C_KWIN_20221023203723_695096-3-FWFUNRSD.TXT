.DISCUSSION...A strong low pressure system will continue to 
affect the region through Monday. This system will bring much 
colder air, higher humidities, gusty winds, and rain/snow from 
northeast WY to the Black Hills and northwest SD. Some light rain 
and snow is also possible south and east of the Black Hills.

week, with a couple of weak disturbances possibly bringing showers
during the midweek timeframe.

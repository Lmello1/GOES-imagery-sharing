.SYNOPSIS...A frontal system will move through the park Friday
evening. Cool upper level trough moving in behind the front later
Friday night with the trough remaining over the park Saturday. 
Weak upper level ridge nosing into the park Saturday night and 
Sunday. Next system coming down the British Columbia coast 
arriving early Monday. Another system will arrive Tuesday night. 
After a brief break a warm front will brush by Wednesday night 
with the trailing cold front moving into the area late Thursday 
into Friday.
